# Marketing Campaign Performance & ROI Analysis

## 📌 Problem

Businesses invest heavily in marketing campaigns but often lack clarity on which channels and strategies actually drive ROI.

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze marketing campaign performance and identify:

* High ROI channels
* Cost-efficient campaigns
* Wasteful spending areas
* Audience targeting effectiveness

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* SQL (MySQL) – Data extraction & analysis
* Power BI – Interactive dashboard & visualization
* Python (Pandas) – Data cleaning & feature engineering

## 📊 Key Metrics

* ROI (Return on Investment)
* CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
* CTR (Click-through Rate)
* Total Spend & Clicks

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

* Channel efficiency using cost vs ROI scatter plots
* Campaign performance comparison
* Audience spend distribution
* Trend analysis of ROI vs cost over time
* Waste campaign detection

## 💡 Key Insights

* ROI remains consistent across channels, indicating stable performance
* High spend campaigns do not significantly outperform others
* Significant opportunity to optimize cost efficiency
* Multiple campaigns identified with high cost but low ROI

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Reallocate budget toward high ROI, low cost channels
* Optimize or eliminate inefficient campaigns
* Improve targeting strategies for better conversion efficiency
